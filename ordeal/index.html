Why Ordeal exists

Every dollar goes to Bo.

My nephew Bo Meier is a 20-year-old student athlete who suffered a severe traumatic brain injury in May 2025. He needs 24/7 care for the foreseeable future, and the costs his family faces go far beyond what insurance covers.

I built Ordeal because I needed it, and because $2.99 from the fitness community adds up to something real. No platform fees. No middleman. Proceeds go directly to Bo's family.

Follow his recovery. Share his story. Every download helps.
